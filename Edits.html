My Edits

For as long as I knew how to use a computer I was obsessed with video editing. I would gather clips of anything I could find to put together. Once I got into skiing and anything else worth pointing a camera at- I was even more obsessed with it.

All of these edits were done using Adobe Premiere Pro

These playbacks are heavily compressed to fit in the github repo. Full quality videos are on my instagram.

I make all of these for fun, and most of them are posted to my instagram. These are the ones I feel are worth sharing here.
