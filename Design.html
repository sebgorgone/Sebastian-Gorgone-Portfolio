Animation Projects

Digital Media and Design design lab animation project

This project was my first ever animation. Working with another classmate, we made this 10 second, 24fps, frame by frame animation. We worked only with the onion layers on Adobe Animate to draw each frame. I was tasked with the second half of the animation where the worm goes into the apple, and then gets blown up.

Digital Media and Design foundation animation for final project

For my Digital Media and Design Foundations final project it had been a requirement to make an animation as part of a design portfolio. I decided to do a frame by frame animation of a skydiving crash video. I exported each frame individually out of seperate illustrator files over the course of 2 weeks. The result is this 10 second, 12 frame per second animation. In hindsight I had been in way over my head trying to do an animation like this using illustrator, and the concequences had been a lack quality control for sure.
